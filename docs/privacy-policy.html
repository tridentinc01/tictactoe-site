👋 1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how TicTacToe: Classic Board Game ("we", "our", or "the app"), developed by Trident Inc, handles information when you use our Android application.

We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy describes what data is collected, how it is used, and what choices you have.

The app itself does not collect any personally identifiable information. No account registration or login is ever required to play.

📱 2. Data Stored Locally on Your Device

The following data is stored only on your device using Android SharedPreferences and is never transmitted to any server or third party:

  • Game scores (wins, draws, losses)
  • Selected visual theme preference
  • Sound on / off preference
  • AI difficulty preference
  • Language preference

All of this data is permanently deleted when you uninstall the app.

📷 3. Camera Access (AR Mode)

The app includes an optional Augmented Reality (AR) Board Mode powered by Google ARCore. When you use AR mode the app requests access to your device camera.

  • Camera access is used solely to render the AR game board on real surfaces
  • No camera images or video are recorded, stored or transmitted
  • Camera permission is only requested when you tap "Play in Augmented Reality"
  • AR mode is entirely optional — the app works fully without it
Camera data never leaves your device. It is processed in real time by ARCore on-device only and is never uploaded or shared.

📢 4. Advertising (Google AdMob)

This app uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and use data to serve personalised or non-personalised ads depending on your consent choices.

Data AdMob may collect includes:

  • Advertising ID — a resettable device identifier
  • Approximate location — derived from IP address for regional ad targeting
  • App interaction data — for ad performance analytics
  • Device information — for ad compatibility
📍 For users in the European Economic Area (EEA) and United Kingdom, we display a GDPR consent form before showing any ads. You can change your consent at any time via "Ad Privacy Settings" inside the app.

For more information on how Google uses advertising data visit: policies.google.com/technologies/ads

🔒 5. Data Security

All data transmitted between the app and third-party services including Google AdMob is encrypted using HTTPS / TLS. Local data stored on your device is protected by Android's built-in application sandboxing.

👶 6. Children's Privacy

This app is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. Our advertising is configured with tagForChildDirectedTreatment set to false.

If you believe a child has provided personal information please contact us at trident.inc01@gmail.com and we will take appropriate action.

🗑 7. Data Deletion

You can delete all data associated with this app in two ways:

  • Uninstall the app — permanently removes all locally stored data immediately
  • Reset Advertising ID — go to device Settings → Privacy → Ads → Delete advertising ID to remove AdMob's identifier

🔗 8. Third-Party Services

This app integrates the following third-party services, each governed by their own privacy policies:

  • Google AdMob — advertising
  • Google Play Services — app distribution and crash reporting
  • Google User Messaging Platform (UMP) — GDPR consent management
  • Google ARCore — Augmented Reality (optional feature)

📋 9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted at this URL with an updated "Last updated" date.

Continued use of the app after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
